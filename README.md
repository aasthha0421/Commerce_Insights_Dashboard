### **Customer Segmentation**
Groups users into Premium, Regular and Budget cohorts, tracking average order value, frequency and retention. Helps identify high-value segments and at-risk users.

### **Retention & Churn Analysis**
Highlights churn drivers with delivery time emerging as the strongest predictor. Visualises repeat rates and drop-off patterns.

### **Competitive Benchmarking**
Compares pricing with Blinkit, Zepto and BigBasket. Identifies under-priced and over-priced items, margin risks and competitive gaps.

### **Profitability Model**
Includes an interactive contribution margin model with adjustable delivery costs, discounts and demand multipliers, allowing you to test various business scenarios.

### **Tier-2 Expansion Assessment**
Evaluates the potential of entering Indore, Bhopal and Coimbatore. Reviews demand indicators, fulfilment cost and projected profitability. Recommends a micro-warehouse strategy with an estimated 8â€“10% margin improvement.

### **Strategic Recommendations**
Summarises four prioritised actions focused on improving retention, reducing discount burn, optimising pricing and expanding into select Tier-2 cities.
